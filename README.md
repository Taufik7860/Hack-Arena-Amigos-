## Project: ClimateSense

**Problem:** Corporate offices and large facilities waste a massive amount of electricity by continuously cooling empty or partially occupied rooms. Facility managers lack real-time, granular visibility into room-by-room energy consumption and struggle with the manual, time-consuming process of compiling accurate data for Environmental, Social, and Governance (ESG) compliance reporting.

**Solution:** **ClimateSense** is an AI-powered Smart Office IoT dashboard designed to optimize HVAC energy usage across a mesh network of rooms. By ingesting live telemetry (temperature and occupancy data), the system dynamically adjusts cooling states in real-time to eliminate energy waste. The centralized admin platform empowers facility managers to monitor live room topology, track lifetime kWh savings and instantly generate one-click ESG compliance reports and monthly raw CSV data exports.
